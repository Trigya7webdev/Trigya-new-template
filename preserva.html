Preserva Wellness

Preserva Wellness Offers A Premium Range Of Ayurvedic Supplements For Daily Use By Harnessing The Power Of The Golden Herb- Turmeric.

The innovative idea of using Ayurveda for benefitting human lives brought forward the brand called Preserva Wellness launched by Trigya Health Products Pvt. Ltd. The diverse range of products are popularised under the name of Preserva Wellness & has gained a place in the hearts of people due to the powerful healing properties. Preserva Wellness is the first brand ever to bring the benefits of curcumin, an extract of turmeric, in the form delicious juices, teas & simple tablets.

With the perfect blend of herbs & spices, premium range of healthcare products focuses on almost all the health needs of the people. Preserva Wellness embraces the power of herbs & believes that purity is everything.

Preserva seeks to be a necessary addition to one’s healthy and wholesome lifestyle while adhering to the principles of traditional Indian medicinal practices, specifically Ayurveda, the literal translation of which is ‘the science of life’.
